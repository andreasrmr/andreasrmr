Biography

About

Neumann, a burgeoning DJ and producer from Denmark, specializes in techno infused with deep basslines, psychedelic synths, and melancholic melodies. His music spans from the brooding tones of 126 BPM dark minimal to the pulsating energy of 132 BPM peak time techno.

Since 2021, Neumann has made his mark on the global techno scene, releasing music on esteemed labels worldwide. His discography boasts notable tracks such as "The Curse," "Shutdown" featuring Fade, and the "Diminish EP" in collaboration with compatriot Bendtsen.

Neumann's performances have graced renowned venues such as Sisyphos and Kit Kat in Berlin, as well as underground raves in downtown LA. He has shared stages with esteemed artists including Victor Ruiz, Thomas Schumacher, Anna Reusch, Cristian Craken, Kaufmann, and other notable talents. With a plethora of unreleased music awaiting its debut, Neumann remains poised to continue his ascent in the electronic music landscape.
